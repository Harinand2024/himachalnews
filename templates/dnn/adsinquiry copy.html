Voices of UAE is a special initiative by DXB News Network, inviting people living in the UAE to share real stories that matter — your struggles, achievements, turning points, or lessons that can inspire others. You don’t need to be a writer or public figure. If your story has honesty and impact, we want to hear it — in any language, with just a clear photo and your words.

To submit, simply fill out our online form and pay a refundable AED 220 registration fee. If your story is not selected, you’ll get a full refund within 7 days. If approved and published, the fee becomes non-refundable. Stories must be respectful, non-promotional, and follow UAE media ethics. This is your chance to be featured on a trusted platform. Your voice can move someone — let it be heard.

A non-refundable registration fee of AED 220 will be required, payable to Markazzia Solutions LLC-FZ.

Participants must provide a deposit slip as proof of payment along with their submissions.

Verification Process

Submissions will undergo basic verification by DXB News Network to ensure authenticity. Participants must provide supporting documents or evidence (e.g., certificates, work samples, testimonials) for their claims.
